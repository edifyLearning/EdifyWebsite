To ensure quality education for children, we must acknowledge that the primary hindrance to learning stems from an education system designed with a one-size-fits-all approach, neglecting diverse learning styles. Our Mission and Solution

How We Can Help

Designing An Online Learning Platform

We will create Edify, A website/tool that is integrated into a service such as e-class or is a standalone tool that is created with the idea of every individual learning on their own terms and through their own learning habits.

Optimizing The Learning Platform For Privacy

Encryption, and access control softwares. Edify collects only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the service. We avoid storing sensitive information that is not required for the core functionality of Edify.

Providing Opportunities For Students

Implementing an AI-driven personalized learning and predictive analytics through Edify to better improve a student's grades / learning habits.
